# PART 1: NOVEL OVERVIEW (ဝတ္တုအကျဉ်းချုပ်)

## Basic Info
- **Title:** 求魔 (Qiú Mó) — "Beseech the Devil" / English: **Pursuit of the Truth** (PoT)
- **Author:** 耳根 (Ěr Gēn), real name 刘勇 (Liú Yǒng). Born in Mudanjiang, Heilongjiang Province. Platinum-level Qidian author.
- **Original title:** Beseech the Devil (求魔) — renamed to **Pursuit of the Truth** per author's wishes.
- **Serialized:** 2012 – December 2013 on Qidian.com
- **Length:** 1,481 chapters, 7 books (~4.5 million Chinese characters)
- **Genre:** Xianxia (仙侠), Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Martial Arts, Mystery, Tragedy
- **Medium:** Web novel
- **Publisher:** Qidian (起点)
- **English translation:** Mogumoguchan (completed January 6, 2020), with editors DarkGem and Zenobys
- **Alternative names:** Beseech the Devil, BTd, Cầu Ma, POT, Qiú Mó, 求魔, สู่วิถีอสุรา
- **Core philosophy:** "三千年叩首，亦或其他。宁做凡人，不为仙人回首。只为她，我不惜一切。" — "Three thousand years bowing to the Devil... I would rather be a mortal than a celestial being when looking back, but for her, I will sacrifice everything."
- **Third novel** of Er Gen, after **Renegade Immortal** (仙逆) and before **I Shall Seal the Heavens** (我欲封天).
- **Er Gen's own assessment:** ~70% satisfied. He described the writing experience as a "change of heart" (化凡 → 化神 → 婴变 stage of his writing career).

## Er Gen Universe (ဧရဂင်宇宙)
Pursuit of the Truth shares a multiverse with Er Gen's other works:
- **Renegade Immortal** (仙逆 Xiān Nì, 2009–2012) — 1st novel
- **Pursuit of the Truth** (求魔 Qiú Mó, 2012–2013) — 2nd novel (this one)
- **I Shall Seal the Heavens** (我欲封天 Wǒ Yù Fēng Tiān, 2014–2016) — 3rd novel
- **A Will Eternal** (一念永恒 Yī Niàn Yǒng Héng, 2016–2019) — 4th novel
- **A World Worth Protecting** (三寸人间, 2019–2021) — 5th novel
- **Beyond the Timescape** (光阴之外, 2022–ongoing) — 6th novel

Su Ming (PoT) is connected to Wang Lin (RI) and Meng Hao (ISSTH) through the Old Man/玄葬 (Xuán Zàng) figure. Power scaling debates place Su Ming at or near Wang Lin's level.

## Author's End Note Summary
Er Gen wrote an emotional end note on the 7th day after the novel ended:
- "Pursuit is in itself a manner of living, bound to be filled with hardships and desolation. Seeking the truth is an attitude, bound to make a person cold and determined."
- Su Ming's story: "When he knew who he was, he was no longer himself, and when he no longer knew who he was, only then was he himself."
- The "Devil" (魔) in the title is not about being a villain — it represents chasing an extreme Dao.
- Those who sever their past = "the only person left in the universe in the end"
- Those who sever their future = "the only one who will disappear from the universe"
- Su Ming's choice is also Er Gen's choice.

# PART 2: SYNOPSIS (ဇာတ်လမ်းအကျဉ်းချုပ်)

An eternal prison, a soulless body, a sealed spirit, everything lost. To resign before merciless fate, or to become destiny!

"I lived in an illusion, I was lost, I could not find my home, I did not have a home… but why does it matter?! Between bowing to the threat of death and a spine that would never bend, I will choose the latter!"

Su Ming grew up dreaming about becoming a Berserker even though he knew that the chances of him becoming one were close to nil. One day, he found a strange piece of debris (black stone fragment), and it allowed him to walk the path of becoming a Berserker. But would it be enough for Su Ming to become just another Berserker to protect those he cares about? Would he be satisfied with leaving everything in fate's hands?

# PART 4: COSMOLOGY & PHILOSOPHY (စကြဝဠာဗေဒနှင့် ဒဿန)

## The Cosmic Structure

### Vast Expanse (浩瀚)
The outermost known universe.

### Harmonious Morus Alba (桑相 Sāng Xiàng)
An enormous **butterfly-shaped universe**. The central cosmological entity of PoT.
- Four wings: two large, two small.
- **Right large wing:** Harmonious Morus Alba Expanse Cosmos
- **Left large wing:** Arid Triad Expanse Cosmos (where Su Ming's story takes place)
- **Small left wing:** Saint Defier's and Dark Dawn's 360 Expanse Cosmoses
- When its wings overlap, one **aeon** (纪) ends — complete destruction of all civilizations.
- Possesses thoughts and emotions similar to humans.
- Has **Children of Morus Alba** (桑相之子) — special beings connected to its will.
- **Yin Death Vortex** and **Bright Yang Vortex** exist between the wings, connecting them like mirrors.

### Arid Triad Expanse Cosmos (三荒大界 Sān Huāng Dà Jiè)
The main setting. Contains:
- **Lower Realms (earth):** Nine Great True Worlds
- **Higher Realms (sky):** Three Great Ancient Kingdoms

Current Great True Worlds:
1. **True Morning Dao World** (真道晨界) — primary setting
2. **Emperor of Abyss' True World** (塑冥皇界)
3. **True Sacred Yin World** (真圣阴界)
4. **True Spirit Hell World** (真狱罗界)

### Yin Death Region (陰死之虛 Yīn Sǐ Zhī Xū)
A mysterious vortex within True Morning Dao World. Contains the **Land of Berserkers** and many other worlds. A mirror passage to Harmonious Morus Alba Expanse Cosmos lies in its depths.

## Key Philosophy: Severing Past vs. Severing Future
- **Severing the past:** You become the only person left in the universe in the end. (You survive but everyone you love is gone.)
- **Severing the future:** You disappear from the universe, but those you love live on.
- Su Ming's choice defines the ending.

# PART 6: CULTIVATION SYSTEM — COMPLETE DETAIL (ကျင့်စဉ်အဆင့်များ အသေးစိတ်)

## Overview
In PoT, cultivation is about mending flaws. Various races have different flaws, and cultivation mends them. Fewer flaws = stronger. The core is **Truth** (真).

Each race has its own system, but a standard accepted by the four Great True Worlds emerged over time. The Berserker path is unique to the Land of Berserkers.

## "Cultivation is killing, seizing, snatching."

---

## THE FIRST STEP (修仙 第一步)

### Berserker Path

#### 1. Blood Solidification (凝血 Níng Xuè)
- **Description:** Entry level. Solidify blood veins to strengthen the body. Uses blood veins (血脉) as the foundation.
- **Sub-levels:** Multiple levels (at least 10).
- **Su Ming's start:** Failed first initiation, then succeeded via black stone fragment.
- **Berserkers at this stage:** Average tribespeople. Small tribes have few.

#### 2. Awakening (开魂 Kāi Hún)
- **Description:** Berserker "awakens" their soul power. Body transforms significantly. Hair may change color.
- **Significance:** Even mid-scale tribes rarely have an Awakened Berserker.
- **Su Ming:** Hair turned purple during Awakening.

#### 3. Bone Sacrifice (祭骨 Jì Gǔ)
- **Sub-levels:** Initial (初), Middle (中), Late (后).
- **Description:** Sacrifice/temper bones to gain power. Physical body undergoes drastic refinement. Su Ming became more slender, skin turned crystal-clear.
- **Significance:** Large-scale tribes possess Bone Sacrifice Berserkers.

#### 4. Berserker Soul (蛮魂 Mán Hún)
- **Sub-levels:** Initial, Middle, Late, Great Completion.
- **Description:** The soul becomes the primary power source. Equivalent to Soul Formation through Ascendant in Immortal cultivation.
- **Great Completion** is a transitional level bridging to the Second Step.
- **Significance:** Only great tribes (Mio Mao, Freezing Sky) have Berserker Soul level Berserkers.

## THE SECOND STEP (修仙 第二步)

### Berserker / Standard Path

#### 5. Life Matrix (命格 Mìng Gé) / Man Realm (人境)
- **Description:** Cultivator begins to understand and manipulate the matrix of life itself.
- **Standard name:** Man Realm (Human Realm)

#### 6. Life Privation (夺命 Duó Mìng) / Earth Realm (地境)
- **Description:** Taking/stealing life force. Understanding of life deepens.
- **Standard name:** Earth Realm
- **Sub-levels:** Initial, Middle, Late, Great Completion.

#### 7. Life Palace (命宫 Mìng Gōng) / Heaven Realm (天境)
- **Description:** Building an internal "palace" of life power. Near-peak of Second Step.
- **Standard name:** Heaven Realm
- **Sub-levels:** Initial, Middle, Late, Great Completion.

#### 8. World of Life (生之世界)
- **Description:** Creating an internal world of life force.
- **Equivalent:** Peak Heaven Realm

#### 9. World Plane (位界 Wèi Jiè)
- **Description:** Cultivator absorbs **Essences of Worlds** (世界精髓). Becomes a **Lord of a World Plane** (位界之主).
- **Sub-levels:**
  - Initial: up to 5,000 Essences
  - Middle: 5,000–10,000 Essences
  - Late: ~10,000 Essences
  - Great Completion: merge 10,000 Essences into one
- **Abilities:** Words become law. Can alter world operations in their area.
- **Limited spots:** Each world has fixed number of World Paragon positions.
- **Equivalent:** Nirvana Shatterer in Immortal cultivation

# PART 13: THEMES & PHILOSOPHY (အကြေးဇူးရည်နှင့် ဒဿများ)

1. **Pursuit (求):** The central action. Pursuing truth, identity, belonging. The journey IS the meaning.
2. **Identity:** "When he knew who he was, he was no longer himself, and when he no longer knew who he was, only then was he himself." Su Ming's identity is continuously stripped and rebuilt.
3. **Fate vs Free Will:** Di Tian and the cosmic cycles represent deterministic fate. Su Ming represents defiance.
4. **Memory and Attachment:** Su Ming's obsession with his past and memories. Memories are his bottom line. Altering them triggers rage.
5. **Loss and Grief:** Everyone Su Ming loves dies or is taken. The Arid Disaster kills them all. His response is rampage.
6. **Sacrifice:** The final choice — disappear so others can live. True power is giving up everything.
7. **The Devil (魔):** Not evil, but extreme pursuit. An attitude of cold determination.
8. **Truth (真):** Cultivating truth = cultivating self. Endless seeking of Dao.
9. **Solitude:** "The only person awake when everyone else was drunk." Su Ming's eternal loneliness.
10. **Reincarnation:** 36+ cycles of life, death, and rebirth. The cycle of suffering and growth.
11. **Manipulation:** Su Ming's entire life was orchestrated by Su Xuan Yi, Di Tian, and Old Man Extermination. His journey is breaking free from all manipulations.

# PART 14: CULTURAL & TRANSLATION NOTES (ယဉ်ကျေးမှုနှင့် ဘာသာပြန်မှတ်ချက်များ)

## Terminology Notes
- **Berserker (蛮 Mán):** Also translates as "barbarian" or "wild." Represents primal, raw power. Not just rage — includes deep connection to nature and ancestry.
- **Devil (魔 Mó):** In PoT context, it means pursuing an extreme Dao, not evil. The title "Beseech the Devil" = seeking the ultimate path.
- **Abyss Builder (塑冥 Sù Míng):** 塑 = shape/mold/build. 冥 = dark/underworld/abyss. Literally "shapers of the abyss." Note: the pinyin of the protagonist's name (苏铭 Sū Míng) is the same pinyin as the race name, which is intentional wordplay.
- **Morus Alba (桑相):** White mulberry tree. The butterfly is named after it. Symbolizes cycles of life (silkworms eat mulberry leaves → spin cocoons → transform).

## Key Differences from Renegade Immortal
- **More tragic:** PoT is considered Er Gen's darkest work. The ending is more bitter-sweet.
- **Slower start:** Er Gen himself noted readers found the beginning slow due to Berserker-focused worldbuilding.
- **Heavier tone:** Oppressed and depressing atmosphere throughout.
- **Identity-focused:** While RI is about defying fate, PoT is about discovering and holding onto identity.
- **Multiple personalities:** Su Ming develops distinct personalities, unlike Wang Lin's single-track determination.

- **Names:** Keep in pinyin. Su Ming, Di Tian, Bai Ling, etc.
